# How to Play

* Press 'A' to move the falling column left.
* Press 'D' to move the falling column right.
* Press 'S' to move the falling column downwards.
* Press 'W' to rotate the order of the three gems in the column.
* Press 'P'` to pause and unpause the game.
* Press 'R' after a game over to reset the game and start again.
* At any time to quit the game by pressing 'Q'.

## Objective

The goal is to line up 3 or more gems of the same colour:

* Horizontally
* Vertically
* Diagonally

When a match is made, those gems disappear and the gems above them fall down due to gravity. This can create chain reactions and increase your score.

## Features Implemented

* Gravity causes the falling column to automatically move down.
* Gravity speed increases as the score gets higher.
* A game over screen appears when the gems reach the top of the playing area.
* A score counter is displayed on the screen.
* A high score counter is displayed and persists until the program is restarted.
* A preview of the next column is shown on the side of the screen.
* Chain reactions award additional points.
